Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes the world has ever known. Few have tried it and lived to tell bout it, and these are thier stories.
We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as a result of consuming Magic Cake is your responsibility. You've been warned, and you can view our legal page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 poung of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Directions

  1. Mix eggs, sugar, and butters in a large bowl
  2. Spread into a non-stick dish
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
  4. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
  5. Eat and enjoy!!!
